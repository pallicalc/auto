Preparation

Liquids & Tablets
  • Liquids: Use an oral syringe. Do NOT use a spoon.
  • Tablets: Remove from package with dry hands.
  • Dissolving: Place tablet in 5ml syringe, add 1ml water + 1ml air, shake.
Ampoules

Snap top off (away from you). Draw medication with needle.

REMOVE NEEDLE BEFORE GIVING

Administration

1
Wash Hands

Wash and dry your hands thoroughly before and after handling the medicine.

2
Place Medicine

Pull back one side of the mouth. Place medicine (liquid or tablet) between the gum and inner cheek.

3
Hold & Position

Patient should stay still until absorbed. If they cannot hold it, lay them on their side (cheek facing down) and place medicine in the lower cheek.

Buccal Administration

Home Care Protocol

Preparation

Liquids & Tablets

Use oral syringe (no spoons). Handle tablets with dry hands. Avoid using hot water for dissolving.

Ampoules

Snap top away from you. Draw up medication.

REMOVE NEEDLE from syringe before giving.

Administration Procedure

1. Clean Hands

Wash and dry hands before handling medication.

2. Place Medicine

Pull back mouth. Place medicine between gum and inner cheek.

3. Positioning

Stay still until absorbed. If unable to hold, lie on side with cheek facing down.
