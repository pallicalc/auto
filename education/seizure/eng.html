Immediate Actions

1. Keep Calm & Note Time

During the episode, stay calm. Take note of when the seizure started.

2. Position Sideways

Turn the patient to their side (recovery position) to help breathing.

Safety Warning

DO NOT put items in mouth

Never place fingers, spoons, or objects into the mouth.

Medication Protocol

1
Administer 1st Dose
Midazolam 1ml(5mg)/Lorazepam 1mg

Give immediately via syringe into the cheek (buccal). If seizures persist, inform your palliative care team in charge.

2
After 10 Minutes

If fitting continues after 10 mins:

2nd Dose: Midazolam 1ml(5mg)/Lorazepam 1mg
3
After Next 10 Mins (20 Total)

If fitting persists:

3rd Dose: Midazolam 1ml(5mg)/Lorazepam 1mg

Call Ambulance Immediately

Record & Inform

Always record the duration of the seizure, the time, and the doses administered. Inform the doctor.

Seizure Management Protocol

Patient Care Guide

Immediate Actions

1. Keep Calm & Note Time

During the episode, stay calm. Take note of when the seizure started.

2. Position Sideways

Turn the patient to their side (recovery position) to help breathing.

Safety Warning

DO NOT put items in mouth

Never place fingers, spoons, or objects into the mouth.

Medication Protocol (Buccal Midazolam)

1. Administer 1st Dose

Midazolam 1ml(5mg)/Lorazepam 1mg via cheek. If seizures persist, inform your palliative care team.

2. After 10 Minutes

If fitting continues: 2nd Dose Midazolam 1ml(5mg).

3. After Next 10 Mins (20 Total)

If fitting persists: 3rd Dose. CALL AMBULANCE IMMEDIATELY.

4. Record & Inform

Record duration, time, and doses. Inform doctor.
